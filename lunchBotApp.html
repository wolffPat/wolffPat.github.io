Features

This is a app to help you find a place to eat and FAST!

it allows you to search for food based on the cuisine and sort it by rating, distance, or price

it will compile a list of 50 places and randomly print the list out item by item.

simply click a button and find a place to eat!

There is also a database so you can create an account and store all your favorite resteraunts!
